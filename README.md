# Insolation_generating_rhythmites_by_imposing_threshold
We provide Python code for generating dynamic visualizations of insolation time series at the 65°N, based on Laskar (2004) solution for the past 10 Myr, across a continuum of threshold values, together with simulated rank and couplet time series and their cross-spectral coherence and phase relationships at the 405-kyr periodicity.
We use "Input_insolation_time_series_La2004.csv" as the sole input data file for generating the rank and couplet time series.
We employ this Jupyter Notebook to simulate rank and couplet time series for each threshold model. This p calculates key model parameters, including the proportion of rank 1 states in the rank time series, the average couplet duration, and the cross-spectral coherence and phase relationships between the rank and couplet series at the 405-kyr periodicity. The results are saved in four output CSV files.
We present the proportion of rank 1 states, average couplet duration, and cross-spectral coherence and phase relationships between the rank and couplet series at the 405-kyr periodicity for each threshold model. The results are displayed as a figure in PNG format.
We also visualize the rank and couplet time series across the threshold continuum within the insolation series, together with the cross-spectral coherence and phase relationships between the rank and couplet series at the 405-kyr periodicity. The results are displayed as an animation in GIF format
